Ulysses 31

Ulysses 31 was a joint project by animation studios in France, Japan, and Luxembourg to bring the legend of Ulysses and his adventure in the Odyssey into the 31st century with a sci-fi twist.

Original Release: Oct 3, 1981

Episode Total: 26

Studios: DiC Audiovisuel, Tokyo Movie Shinsha, France 3, RTL
