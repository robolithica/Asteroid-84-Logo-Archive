# Cartoon-Logo-Database
An archive of logos for cartoons, toylines, and comic books the 1980s.

My love of logo design goes back as far as I can remember, as does my love of the cartoons, toys, and comics that are now synonymous with my childhood. Add to that my drive to collect, categorize, and archive things (which is probably written in my dna), and you have the impetus for Asteroid-84. 

I enjoyed recreating these logos with as much detail as I can muster and attempt in every instance to
remain true to the original designs. I hope you enjoy seeing these cleaned up versions as much as I do and
I hope they conjure the same nostalgia and peace of mind that I received while making them.
Thanks!
