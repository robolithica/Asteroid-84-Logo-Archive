Beverly Hills Teens

An obvious attempt to bring the popularity of Beverly Hills 90210 to the 7-10 year old demographic, Beverly Hills Teens was about a group of limitlessly wealthy high school students and all the pitfalls of their social lives, with at least one character representing every cartoon character trope imaginable.

Original Release: Sept 19, 1987
